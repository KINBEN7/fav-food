JOLLOF RICE

Go To Ingredients

Go To Methods

Jollof rice is one of my favourite foods.Here is a recipe for a delicious jollof rice.

Ingredients

  • 450g easy-cook long grain rice
  • 500g fresh vine tomatoes.
  • 2 red bell pepper chopped.
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper, deseeded.
  • 1 white or red onion, chopped.
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • thumb sized fresh ginger.
  • 1 tbsp. tomato puree
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp onion granuels
  • 1 tbsp garlic granuels
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose seasoning
  • 1 tbsp. oil
  • 350ml beef stock
  • Salt, black pepper, additional all-purpose seasoning, to taste

Method

  1. Wash the peppers, onion, and tomatoes.
  2. Blend the chopped ingredients together.
  3. Rinse the rice in water until it runs clear.
  4. Heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  5. Chop 1 onion and saute it in the oil for 4 minutes
  6. Cook the tomato paste for 5 minutes
  7. Add in the pepper puree and spices.
  8. Add the rice, chicken stock, water, and salt.
  9. Cover and cook the rice for 20 min.
  10. Simmer the rice for an additional 10-20 min
  11. Fluff and serve the jollof rice.
